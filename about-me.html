About Me.

Hello! I’m Francis Borja, and I study Computer Science at
Stevens Institute of Technology in Hoboken NJ.

I grew up in North Jersey, 10 minutes away from New York City, and have had a growing interest in design for many years. I hope to carry on this desire to create things through web design and development.

I’m a fan of several New York sports teams, most notable the New York Knicks, and the New York Giants. I am also an avid fan of esports, including Counter Strike: Global Offensive, and Super Smash Brothers Melee for the Nintendo Gamecube.

You can expect to see me post blog posts about several topics, including technology, sports, Esports, videogames, or my own personal thoughts.

Thank you for visiting my website!
